Spirited Away Characters

No-Face

No-Face (カオナシ Kaonashi, lit. "Faceless") is a spirit and a secondary antagonist in the Studio Ghibli film Spirited Away. He is shown to be capable of reacting to emotions and ingesting other individuals in order to gain their personality and physical traits.

Chihiro, Sen

Chihiro (荻野 千尋 Ogino Chihiro), referred throughout most of the film by Sen (千 lit. "one-thousand"), is the ten-year-old core protagonist of the Japanese animated film Spirited Away.
